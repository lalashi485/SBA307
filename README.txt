The idea behind "Red Books" is to be a website that helps book collectors easily identify special edition books and where to purchase them from. The pages included in this project are a sample membership form, user dashboard, and admin dashboard. 

Running the project:
Click on any of the provided .html files and they will open in your preferred web browser. Chrome is highly recommended.

Technical information:

Bootstrap and Google fonts were linked to enhance the appearance of the site. Flexbox was used in order to affect how containers looked and were placed.
